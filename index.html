Bridgewalk is a 2D endless runner arcade game that is made using Unity and its inspired by the 3D game PLANK! In this game, the player has to keep on building bridges to land on to the next platform and if the bridge is too long/short or if the bridge isn't built in time, the player falls and dies. As the player crosses successfully, another platform pops up and it varies in distance from the player.
